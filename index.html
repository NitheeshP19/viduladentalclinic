Treatments Offered

Comprehensive dental solutions for everyday care and smile confidence.

01

General Checkups

At Vithula Dental Clinic, we believe prevention is the foundation of a healthy smile. Our routine dental exams are thorough, pain-free, and designed to catch problems early — before they become costly. Using advanced diagnostic tools, we assess your gum health, check for cavities, and review your overall oral hygiene with a personalised care plan tailored to you.

Routine Exam Cavity Detection Oral Cancer Screening X-Ray Analysis
Before After
02

Teeth Cleaning

At Vithula Dental Clinic, we provide highly advanced and completely painless professional cleaning treatments. Our ultrasonic scaling removes stubborn tartar and plaque that regular brushing can't reach — leaving your teeth sparkling clean, your breath fresher, and your gums healthier. We use the latest sterilised instruments for a comfortable, safe experience every visit.

Ultrasonic Scaling Plaque Removal Stain Polishing Gum Care
03

Cosmetic Dentistry

At Vithula Dental Clinic, we transform smiles with state-of-the-art cosmetic treatments that are as gentle as they are beautiful. Whether you dream of whiter teeth, perfectly aligned veneers, or a complete smile makeover, our expert team crafts results that look completely natural. Your confidence starts with your smile — and we are here to perfect it.

Teeth Whitening Porcelain Veneers Smile Makeover Bonding
04

Tooth Restoration

At Vithula Dental Clinic, we restore damaged, decayed, or missing teeth using the most advanced materials and minimally invasive techniques available. From tooth-coloured composite fillings to durable dental crowns and implants, every restoration is crafted to look, feel, and function just like your natural teeth — giving you back your smile and your confidence.

Fillings Dental Crowns Implants Root Canal
