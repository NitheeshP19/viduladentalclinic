Who It Helps

Common reasons patients in Theni search for braces and aligners.

Orthodontic treatment is not only about appearance. It can also help improve bite balance, oral hygiene access, and confidence in smiling or speaking.

Crowded or overlapping teeth

When teeth overlap, cleaning becomes harder and the smile may look uneven. Treatment planning helps create better alignment and improved access for hygiene.

Spacing, gaps, or visible misalignment

Some patients want braces or aligners because of uneven gaps, forward teeth, or a smile that does not feel balanced. A consultation helps identify the best correction path.

Bite-related concerns

Improper bite relationships may affect function as well as appearance. Individual assessment is important for deciding whether braces, aligners, or related treatment is appropriate.

What happens during the first braces or aligner consultation

The first visit usually focuses on understanding the alignment problem, examining the teeth and bite, and discussing treatment goals. From there, the dentist can explain whether the case is suitable for braces, aligners, or another smile correction approach, and what the commitment may involve.

  • Assessment of crowding, gaps, bite relationship, and smile alignment.
  • Discussion of the patient's goals, comfort, appearance priorities, and routine.
  • Planning for the most suitable treatment pathway based on the case.
  • Regular follow-up guidance so progress stays on track over time.

FAQ

Questions patients ask about braces and aligners.

Who may need braces or aligners?

They may be recommended for crowding, spacing, protrusion, bite concerns, or visible smile misalignment that needs a structured correction plan.

Are aligners suitable for every patient?

No two cases are the same. A proper examination helps determine whether aligners or braces are a better fit for the specific alignment and bite condition.

How long does treatment take?

The timeline depends on complexity, bite correction needs, and treatment type. An individual consultation gives the clearest estimate.

Can adults also start treatment?

Yes. Many adults seek braces or aligners for smile improvement, spacing issues, crowding, and bite correction with a treatment plan suited to their goals.
